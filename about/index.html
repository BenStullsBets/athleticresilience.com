Our mission

Bring health and performance to everyone.

Athletic Resilience exists to bring durable health and performance to the masses — encouraging formative habits that maintain elite performance and a pain-free lifestyle. We believe the body works in fascial movement patterns that connect you from head to toe, and that the right systems help anyone reach their potential. Be resilient.

The team

Two doctors, one philosophy: Train. Recover. Perform.

Dr. Lee Schuster

Founder · Chiropractor & Athletic Trainer (DC, ATC)

For 15+ years, Dr. Lee has worked with athletes and patients across rugby, football, baseball, hockey, lacrosse, soccer, and basketball — from youth through the professional level. He's a firm believer that we must balance the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of life to continually build, grow, and find joy in our endeavors.

As a certified athletic trainer (ATC), Lee does the hands-on, sideline, and event work — and taping is his calling card. Other ATs and PTs come to him to level up their taping: an evidence-informed method built on fascial slings and movement patterns. It's the skill our testimonials keep singling out, and the foundation of our clinician certification.

Off the clock you'll find him hiking, fishing, camping, on the rugby pitch, wrestling, kickboxing, or exploring the backcountry. His motto: Be Resilient.

Dr. Greg

Co-Founder · Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT)

A Doctor of Physical Therapy with advanced training in sports performance, strength & conditioning, and recovery — and a former Division I collegiate athlete. Dr. Greg focuses on bringing people out of pain and back to what they want to do.

His DPT puts clinical rehab and return-to-play squarely in his wheelhouse, and his work spans the pros down to high school — and the everyday population in between. That range is what widens Athletic Resilience beyond elite sport: the same principles that get a starter back on the field help anyone move better.

His foundational approach applies whether you're an elite athlete or just managing everyday activities around the house. His motto, too: Be Resilient.

Better together

One team across the whole continuum.
